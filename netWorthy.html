Net Worthy

Social "Networthing" App for tracking and sharing your financial journey

Personal Project

Overview

Net Worthy is a social "networthing" app that allows users to track their net worth in real-time by connecting bank accounts, crypto wallets, and more. Share your financial journey with the community on your terms.

The app combines powerful financial tracking tools with social features, enabling users to gain insights into their financial health while optionally sharing their progress with others for accountability and community support.

Key Features

Real-Time Net Worth Tracking

Connect your bank accounts, investment portfolios, crypto wallets, and other financial accounts to see your complete financial picture updated in real-time.

Crypto Wallet Integration

Track your cryptocurrency holdings across multiple wallets and exchanges, with automatic price updates and portfolio analytics.

Social Sharing on Your Terms

Choose what to share and with whom. Whether you want full transparency or prefer to keep things private, you control your financial narrative.

Tech Stack

  • Next.js + React for the frontend
  • Neon serverless Postgres database with Drizzle ORM
  • Plaid for secure bank account connections
  • Anthropic Claude API for AI-powered insights
  • Deployed on Vercel

My Role

As the creator and sole developer of Net Worthy, I handled all aspects of the product from conception to deployment:

  • Designed the product vision and user experience
  • Built the full-stack application using modern web technologies
  • Integrated financial APIs for bank and crypto tracking
  • Implemented AI-powered portfolio insights using Claude
  • Deployed and maintain the production application
