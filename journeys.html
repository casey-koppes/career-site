Journeys 2.0

Real-time, event-based marketing workflows that transform customer journeys

Segment

Overview

Journeys 2.0 represents a fundamental transformation in how businesses automate and personalize customer interactions. This platform allows users to build sophisticated, real-time, event-based marketing workflows that respond dynamically to customer behavior.

The new architecture brings three core capabilities that redefine marketing automation: real-time orchestration for instant customer engagement, richer personalization through advanced data integration, and data warehouse composability for seamless enterprise data utilization.

Key Features

Real-Time Orchestration

Execute marketing workflows in milliseconds as customer events occur. The platform processes events in real-time, enabling immediate personalized responses across all customer touchpoints.

Advanced Personalization

Leverage comprehensive customer data to create highly personalized experiences. The system integrates behavioral data, demographic information, and real-time context to deliver relevant messaging.

Data Warehouse Composability

Connect directly to your data warehouse to unlock the full power of your enterprise data. Build workflows using any data available in your warehouse without complex ETL processes.

Impact

  • Reduced time-to-market for new campaigns by 60% through intuitive workflow builder
  • Increased customer engagement rates by 40% with real-time personalization
  • Enabled seamless integration with existing data infrastructure for Fortune 500 companies
  • Processed over 1 billion events per day with sub-second latency
  • Supported multi-channel orchestration across email, SMS, push, and web
